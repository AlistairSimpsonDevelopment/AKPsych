About Me

Dr Alexandra Kasozi is a chartered Counselling Psychologist working in London. She holds a professional Doctorate in Counselling Psychology (PsychD) and is registered with the Health Care Professional Council (HCPC) and the British Psychological Society (BPS).

Alexandra currently runs a private practice in South West London where she provides counselling and psychological therapy to individuals. She also provides psychological coaching and facilitation to organisations.

In the past, Alexandra has worked as a psychologist in secondary care for several NHS mental health trusts and in charitable and not-for-profit services.
